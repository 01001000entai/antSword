Uninstall Plugins
---

1. Go to 「`local repo`」 under the tab of plugin store.

2. Choose the plugin you want to uninstall, and click 「`uninstall`」 button beneath the plugins.

[][img_uninstall_plugin_1]


3. Click red confirm button.
